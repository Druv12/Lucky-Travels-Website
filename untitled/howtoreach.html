HOW TO REACH ANDAMAN & NICOBAR ISLANDS




By Air

Airport of Andaman & Nicobar is situated at Port Blair the Capital of Andaman & Nicobar Islands which is connected with Chennai and Kolkata by air. Presently flights are operated by Indian Airlines, Jet Airways, Air Deccan, Jetlite are operating flights between Chennai-Port Blair-Chennai sector and Kolkata-Port Blair –Kolkata sector on all 7 days of the week. Jetlite operates flights between Delhi - Port Blair - Delhi via Kolkata on all 7 days of the week.

By Sea

Ship services for regular passenger are available to Port Blair from Chennai, Kolkata and Visakhapatnam and back. There are three to four sailings every month from Kolkata and Chennai to Port Blair and vice-versa. There is only one sailing from Visakhapatnam in a month. The voyage takes about 50 to 60 hours and the ship normally berths at Haddo Port, Port Blair for about two to four days.

HOW TO BUY SHIP TICKETS

The prescribed requisition forms (free of cost) will be available in the Inquiry Counter. The passengers are required to fill up the form and submit it along with two passport size photograph and supporting documents, if any, to avail concessions at the ticketing counter. All passengers are required to give details of infants below 2 years of age while purchasing tickets. No fare will be charged for the infants.

  • To Buy tickets at Chennai

    • The issuance of Passenger tickets and booking of Cargo for MV Nancowry and MV Swaraj Dweep are done at the Counter attached with the office of the Deputy Director (Shipping Services), Chennai adjacent to Gate No.1 of Chennai Port, Opposite Customs Office, Rajaji Salai, Chennai – 1.

    The issuance of Passenger tickets and booking of Cargo for MV Nicobar, MV Akbar and MV Harshavardhana are done at the Counter attached with the office of the SCI, Rajaji Salai, Opposite to Customs House, Chennai - 600 001.


  • To Buy tickets at Kolkata

    • The tickets for all the passenger vessels are issued by Shipping Corporation of India Ltd., from their office situated at 13, Strand Road, Calcutta-17.

  • To Buy tickets at Visakhapatnam

    • The issuance of passage tickets and booking of cargo in respect of all the passenger vessels calling at Visakhapatnam is done by an Agent of SCI M/s AV Bhanoji Rao Garuda Pattabiramaiah & Co. Ltd., at their private ticketing counter
