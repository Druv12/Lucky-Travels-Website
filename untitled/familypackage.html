Family Tour Packages 6 Nights 7 Days


Day 1: Airport pickup and City Tour

Upon arrival in Port Blair in the morning/afternoon, after refreshment .You will be taken to city tour. Overnight stay at respective hotel.

Day 2: Port Blair to Havelock (by Cruise)

Early morning you depart from Port Blair on a Cruise/ferry for Havelock (Swaraj Dweep) which is India’s must visit destination in Andaman Island, upon reaching Havelock Island now known as Swaraj dweep our representative will pick you up and drop you at your hotel/resort After lunch our trip begins to Asia’s best beach Radha Nagar beach. We prefer you choose a beach side hotel/resort. Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 3: Elephant beach (By boat)

Next Day morning have your buffet Breakfast (complementary) and check-out from the hotel/resort vehicle will transfer you to the jetty. From there board ferry which will take approx. 25 min to reach Elephant beach enjoy a complimentary snorkelling on your way to Elephant beach. Vehicle will take you to the hotel, where you can unwind and relax.

Overnight stay at respective Hotel / Resort at Havelock.

Day 4:Havelock to Port Blair (Cruise ride)

Check out from your hotel/resort our representative will pick you up and drop to Havelock jetty from there board a cruise to Port Blair. After reaching Port Blair our representative will pick you up and drop you to hotel/resort where you can unwind and relax.

Day 5:Baratang

On the next morning our Tour Coordinator will be waiting for you to take you to have a visit baratang

Day 6:NSCB(Ross Island) & North Bay Island

On the next morning our Tour Coordinator will be waiting for you to take you to have a visit Ross Island and North Bay Island for a memorable Tour in Andaman.

Day 7: Depature from Port Blair

Depature to airport
